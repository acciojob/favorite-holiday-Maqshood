Favorite holiday destination

Paris, the capital of France, is a world-renowned destination celebrated for its rich history, stunning architecture, and romantic charm. From the iconic Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum to charming cafes and cobblestone streets, Paris offers a perfect blend of culture, cuisine, and timeless elegance that captivates millions of visitors each year.


Top 5 attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A global symbol of France, this iron lattice tower offers breathtaking views of Paris from its upper decks.
  2. Louvre Museum – The world’s largest art museum and home to famous works like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture known for its stunning stained-glass windows and historic significance.
  4. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe – A famous avenue lined with shops and cafes, ending at the monumental Arc de Triomphe.
  5. Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur Basilica – vA charming hilltop district known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and the beautiful white-domed basilica.

Must-try foods

  • Croissant–Flaky, buttery, and best enjoyed fresh from a Parisian bakery in the morning.
  • Baguette –A crisp, golden loaf of French bread that's a staple in nearly every meal.
  • Escargots de Bourgogne –Snails cooked in garlic-parsley butter, a classic French delicacy.
  • Duck Confit (Confit de Canard) – Slow-cooked duck leg with crispy skin, rich in flavor and tradition.
